    The Writings of James Lythgoe

    Two important records kept by James Lythgoe (Darrin's great great grandfather) are now online:

    Missionary Journal. Documents James' travels and experiences in the Manchester, England Conference of the LDS Church, 1862-1863. Includes full scans of all 270 pages.

    Temple Book. Records of temple work performed by James and his friends and family for deceased relatives and acquaintances, late 1800s to early 1900s. Also includes a short histories from James and his son Joseph. Full scans of 57 pages.

    More photos and histories of James Lythgoe. Includes his patriarchal blessing, a few short biographies and his obituary.
