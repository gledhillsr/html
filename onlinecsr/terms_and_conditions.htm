Terms and Conditions for ONLINE-CSR service

ONLINE-CSR, Inc. is an independent company who specializes in the retrieval of records and the placing of orders for the Regional Bell Operating Companies (RBOCs)

ONLINE-CSR is a fee based service.  The fees for the service have been established by your ONLINE-CSR agent or directly by ONLINE-CSR You will be billed by your agent or ONLINE-CSR after your service has been set up.

You may cancel the service within the first 10 days and you will not be charged.
