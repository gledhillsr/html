Classes to be held 6PM to 9+PM at Salt Lake Police Depart. Pioneer precinct, at 700 South and 10th West.



Also required are Assessment and Vital signs sheets to be completed before the end of the course. It is suggested that the class plan some evening and weekends to have "Assessment and Vital signs" parties to help complete these requirements. Also a good time to practice and develop some hands-on-skills as they are learned in class. These can be "pot luck" or go to lunch/dinner after programs. Early in the course the Park makes a good place to meet for these activities. Backyards and other places (Brighton Ski resort?) can be arranged for as well. Bring you spouse, kids, significant other and even friends to act as "patients" for these programs and for them to see what you are learning.



Traditions of the class are to take turns bring a treat etc. for the class. We will pass around a sign-up sheet for this purpose.
