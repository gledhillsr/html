The annual Brighton OEC Refresher will be held next weekend (October 6th). This email contains important details about the Refresher.

If you will NOT be attending the Refresher, please let me know as soon as possible.

Remember to bring these items:
*   Your OEC card
*   A check for $86 written to Brighton Ski Patrol to cover your dues (visiting patrollers please bring $20)
*   Lunch and something to drink

You should also read the 2012 OEC Refresher Workbook. (You can download the 20 meg workbook from
http://beaconreviews.com/transceivers/2012OECWorkbook.pdf
or from NSP.org.)

The Refresher will be held at the same location as last year:

Christ United Methodist Church
3300 South 2375 East
 Salt Lake City, UT 84109

Registration will begin at 7:15 and the Refresher will start at 8:00. Snacks will be provided in the morning; be sure to bring your own lunch.

If the church parking lot fills up, there is overflow parking on the south side of 3300 South, just east of the grass field. Please do not park in the veterinarian's parking area, just west of the church, or on 3300 South.

The Lift Evacuation training will be held on Sunday, October 9th at Brighton. Remember to bring lunch and warm clothes.
