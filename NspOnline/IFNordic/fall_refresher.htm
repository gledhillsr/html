What to bring to the Refresher:

  • Completed IDC test
  • Completed CPR test
  • Completed OEC Refresher Study Guide Test
    You must pass all three tests to patrol.  This requirement will be strictly enforced.  Legibility and neatness count toward a passing grade, just like on accident reports.  Remember too that a completed Refresher Test is your ticket to one station.
  • Your OEC card
    Replacement cards cost $5.00.  You'll need to have this card signed to Patrol.
  • Patrol Coat
    For individual Pictures
  • Lunch for both Saturday and Sunday
  • Warm clothes
    Sitting on that chair list during lift evac can get very cold, remember it has rained and snowed in the past
  • Cash or Check for $63.00
    If you didn't pre-pay, you'll need to pay your dues at the Refresher

Attending Another Refresher?
If you plan to attend another refresher, please fill out the information requested on the Patrol Roster Information form.  Make sure you get signed up wherever you attend and verification comes back to the director.  Completing an Annual Refresher is required to Patrol.
