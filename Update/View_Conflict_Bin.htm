Viewing the Conflict Bin

Conflicts sometimes occur between versions of files during synchronization, as files are exchanged between your workstations and the Novell® iFolderTM server. Instead of deleting or overwriting those files, iFolder saves the files in your Conflict Bin, up to the space available in the bin. When size limitations are exceeded, iFolder deletes the oldest files to make room for the current save process.

You must be logged in to view and manage the files in the Conflict Bin.

Opening the Conflict Bin Viewer

To view the files saved in your iFolder Conflict Bin, you may use either of the following methods:

  • Right-click the iFolder icon, then click View Conflict Bin

  • Right-click the iFolder icon, click Account Information > View > View Conflict Bin

The viewer lists files that were in saved when they were overwritten or deleted during a synchronization.

Setting the Bin Size

In the Conflict Bin Viewer, you can set the maximum space (in megabytes (MB)) you want to reserve for the files in conflict. Remember that this space is part of the total space allocated to you for iFolder storage by your iFolder administrator.

To set the maximum bin size, click File > Properties, enter the maximum space to reserve, then click OK.

Restoring Files in the Conflict Bin

You can restore any of the listed files or permanently delete them from your iFolder directories. To restore files, select the desired files in the Conflict Bin View list, then click Restore. To permanently delete files, select the files for deletion in the Conflict Bin View list, then click Delete.
