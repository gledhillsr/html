Finding Information about iFolder Client

To find information about your Novell® iFolderTM client software, right-click the iFolder icon, then click About iFolder. This opens a window on your desktop that displays the following information:

  • Novell iFolder Client software version

  • Copyright information

  • Licensing information

  • Third-party encryption software acknowledgment

 

A trademark symbol (®, TM, etc.) denotes a Novell trademark. An asterisk (*) denotes a third-party trademark. For more information, see Legal Notices.
