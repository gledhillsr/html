This year's annual refresher will focus on "Cycle A" of the OEC. The refresher will begin at 7:50am October 5th and October 6th 2002 at the Brighton Ski Resort - Alpine Rose Lodge. Each station will be 45 minutes in length with a 5-minute break between. The following information will be a general outline of the weekend's events.

 

Items to bring to the refresher

q       Payment for dues - $58.00 – Credit for the purchase of the OEC manual will be issued for patrollers meeting the required number of days. 16 days full time, 10 days part time

q       OEC card - If lost a $5.00 replacement fee will be charged

q       OEC, CPR books and refresher information

q       OEC, CPR, IDC tests

q       Patrol coat for pictures

q       Lunch for both days

q       Appropriate clothing - Lift evac can be oh, oh so cold when you do not have the correct cloths and shoes.
