Infectious Control Procedure

All members will:

  • Assume ultimate responsibility for their own health and safety
  • Employ BSI on all patients
  • Use appropriate personal protective equipment as situation dictates
  • Report any significant exposure to a communicable disease to their supervisor immediately
  • Report any diagnosis of communicable disease (occupational or non-occupational) to the Infection Control Officer
