Hello Patrollers! I hope you had a wonderful summer and that the Fall weather has you dreaming of snow.

This is a reminder that the annual Refresher will be held the weekend of October 8th and 9th.

The OEC portion will be held on Saturday, October 8th at the same location as last year:

Christ United Methodist Church
3300 South 2375 East
 Salt Lake City, UT 84109

Registration will begin at 7:15 and the Refresher will start at 8:00. Snacks will be provided in the morning; be sure to bring your own lunch.

If the church parking lot fills up, there is overflow parking on the south side of 3300 South, just east of the grass field. Please do not park in the veterinarian's parking area, just west of the church, or on 3300 South.

The Lift Evacuation training will be held on Sunday, October 9th at Brighton. Registration will be from 7:45 to 8:00. Remember to bring lunch and warm clothes.

Before the Refresher, please: 

  1. Locate your OEC card. If you've misplaced it, visit nsp.org, choose Contact from the bottom of the page,  and snail-mail $5 to the NSP, telling them your NSP member number and that you need a replacement OEC card.
  2. Write a check for $86 (unchanged from last year) to Brighton Ski Patrol for your annual dues. You can bring this to the Refresher or mail it to David Tamowski. If you participated in a fund raiser that waives your dues for this year, please send David  an email reminding him.

David Tamowski
 8525 South 1000 East
 Sandy, Utah 84094

Please feel free to contact Laurie or me if you have questions.
