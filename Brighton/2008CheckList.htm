Brighton Refresher Checklist

October 11th and 12th 2008

 

Before The Refresher:

 

—        Locate your OEC card

—      If you cannot find it, please contact the MIST team at the NSP office to order a replacement.

—        Download and complete the CPR/AED test from BrightonNSP.org

—        Read the Refresher Study Guide Cycle A 2008

—      If you misplaced the Guide, it can be downloaded from BrightonNSP.org

—      Complete a written overview of Scenarios #1, #2, and #3

—      Write your own Scenario #4

—      Review the associated chapters in the OEC manual (4th Edition)

—        Visit BrightonNSP.org, choose My Info, and confirm that your information is correct

—        Optionally, you can mail your dues to Dave Tamowski (details, below)

 

Saturday Morning: October 11th New location

Christ United Methodist Church

3300 South 2375 East

Salt Lake City

—        Put your OEC card in the appropriate box

—        Turn in your four scenarios This is your Ticket into the refresher

—        Turn in your completed CPR/AED test (put your name on the answer sheet)

—        Complete and turn in a Board Representative Ballot

(Remember, Auxiliaries vote for Auxiliaries, Basics for Basics, and Seniors for Seniors.)
