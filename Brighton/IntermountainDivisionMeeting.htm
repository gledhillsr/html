2004 INTERMOUNTAIN DIVISION

"JUST DOESNT SUCK"

MEETING

 

The annual fall meeting/banquet/party is for all patrollers in the

Intermountain Division.  At the meeting division policies, procedures,

events and new ideas are reviewed, written and acted upon or voted on.

Elections are scheduled for the Division Director and the two Assistant

Division Directors this year. Your participation is desired and needed!

 

Joining us for the meeting this year will be Bill Sach, the National

Chairman of the NSP, Neil Booth the Division Director from the Southern

Division and Larry Bost National OEC Director has been invited as well.

 

The business meeting will take place Saturday morning and afternoon. The

business meeting will be followed by the IMD banquet and party that

evening.  This year Diamond Peaks Heli-Ski Patrol will be hosting the

event. The banquet/party for this year will have a "Caribbean Theme", with

awards, and raffle for prizes. Charlie, Craig, Tina and the whole Diamond

Peaks crowd indicate that we should come dressed for the “Islands” and be

prepared to “Have Fun”.
